## Maintenance

**Routine:** 1–2 business days response

**Emergency (24/7 response within 1 hour):**
- Water leak/flooding
- No heat (<50°F) or AC (>90°F)
- Gas smell
- Electrical hazard
- Security issues (locks, windows)
